How I Work |

Structured QA process from discovery to delivery — pragmatic testing, automation, and verification to ensure production-ready software.

Identify

Gather requirements, acceptance criteria, and risk areas to define focused testing goals and scope.

test_planning

Research

Analyze system architecture, data flows, and past defects to uncover edge cases and coverage gaps.

Design

Author clear test cases, test data, and automation plans aligned to acceptance criteria and risk.

test_execution

Execute

Execute exploratory, manual, and automated tests (Playwright, Postman, JMeter). Validate behavior and capture logs/traces.

Report

Log reproducible defects with steps, evidence, and severity. Triage and correlate failures to root causes.

quality_assured

Deliver

Verify fixes, run regression suites, confirm release readiness, and sign off with test evidence and metrics.
